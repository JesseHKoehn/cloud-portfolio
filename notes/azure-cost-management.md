# Azure Cost Management Notes

## Free Account Protections
- Spending limit is enabled by default on free accounts
- Azure suspends resources instead of charging when limit is hit
- Cost Alerts UI requires a paid subscription

## Best Practices I Follow
- Always delete resource groups after finishing a lab
- Check Cost Management dashboard after every session
- Never leave VMs or databases running when not in use

## Free Account Limits
- $200 credit for first 30 days
- 12 months of popular free services after that
- Some services are always free
